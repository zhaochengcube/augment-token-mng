# Email

Manage Outlook, GPT mail and iCloud HME email features.

## Features

- **iCloud HME**: Generate, list, deactivate and clean up HME mailboxes (when enabled in app).
- **Outlook**: Add and manage Outlook accounts (if the feature is enabled).
- **GPT Mail**: GPT mail accounts and related actions (if enabled).

Open from the sidebar: **Email**, then choose the service.
